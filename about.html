Our History

A Foundation of Excellence

Established with a vision to revolutionize the Tanzanian construction sector, Oken Engineering Limited has grown from a startup to a fully compliant and trusted engineering firm.

Company Established

Oken Engineering Limited was founded and incorporated in Tanzania. The company began operations with a focus on delivering reliable and high-quality engineering solutions to local clients.

  • Incorporation in Tanzania
  • Initial Project Acquisition
  • Setting Core Values

Regulatory Compliance

We achieved full compliance with Tanzanian regulations, including BRELA registration and Tax Identification (TIN). We secured business licenses for hardware supply and ICT equipment.

  • BRELA Registration
  • Tax Clearance Certificate
  • General Supply Licenses

Professional Registration

A milestone year where Oken Engineering pursued registration with the Contractors Registration Board (CRB) as Building and Civil Contractors, enhancing our capacity to take on larger public and private projects.

  • CRB Building Contractor Class V
  • CRB Civil Contractor Class V
  • Expanded Technical Team

Regional Leader

Today, we serve Government institutions, NGOs, and Corporate organizations. We are recognized for our integrity and quality, delivering sustainable infrastructure solutions across Tanzania.

  • Serving Public & Private Sectors
  • Innovative Civil Works
  • Sustainable Development Focus
